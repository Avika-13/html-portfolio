The Best Movies According to Avika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Cruella

It is one of the BEST VILLIAN ORIGIN STORIES to date. I had so much fun watching that I didn't even see the twist coming, and when it hit, it hit in all the right places and took the whole story on another level.

Bahubali 1 & 2

Bahubali is one of the most visually impressive films that have come from India. Everything about this film is good - Story, direction, beautiful cinematography, haunting score and amazing visuals.

Life of Pi

A glorious film, which caught me off gaurd, blew my mind off and kept me hooked till the very end. A visual masterpiece, and an intoxicating story of belief and friendship. It's a shame that I didn't see this at the cinema.
